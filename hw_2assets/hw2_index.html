About Me

Grant, an Austin native, finished his undergraduate studies at Texas State University with a Bachelor in English. Like many other liberal arts graduates, Grant went through unecessary existential crises trying to figure out what he was going to do with the rest of his life.

After working remotely in digital marketing for the last several years, he decided to take the plunge into coding bootcamp. He hopes to continue working remotely as a full stack developer so he can collaborate on fun, creative projects and make money from anywhere in the world!
