About Me

My professional journey started as an SDR for HotSchedules. In my next role, I did sales for a digital agency, where I decided that SEO was more fulfilling to me than sales. During my time as an SEO, I had to manipulate some HTML and CSS to boost rankings without actually understanding what the code was doing. This is when I started wanting to learn code, and after having a coding bootcamp review aggregator as a client, I was sold on taking the plunge. Here I am!

My background in digital marketing makes me a valuable asset to any development team I join: I am conversion-focused and am able to develop while simultaneously leaving less mess for UX and marketing teams to clean up. Additionally, all of my previously held roles have been client-facing so I am a strong communicator, a versatile teammate and an ambitious learner.

Having finished my undergraduate in English, I could've never guessed life would one day bring me to coding but in the words of Jerry Garcia, "what a long, strange trip it's been!"
